# Dental Practice Management System (DPMS)

A modern, scalable Dental Practice Management System designed for dental clinics and multi-chair practices.

## Features

### Patient Management
- Patient Registration
- Patient History
- Medical & Dental Records
- Document Management

### Appointment Management
- Calendar View
- Dentist Scheduling
- Follow-up Appointments
- Appointment Status

### Dental Billing
- General Billing
- CGHS Billing
- ECHS Billing
- Payment Tracking
- Invoice Generation
- PDF Export
- Print Support

### Treatment Management
- Dental Procedures
- Treatment Plans
- Treatment Packages
- Dental Prescriptions

### Dental Records
- Odontogram (Planned)
- Dental Images (Planned)
- X-Ray Management (Planned)

### Reports
- Revenue Reports
- Patient Reports
- Billing Reports
- Payment Reports

### Administration
- User Management
- Role Based Access Control
- Settings
- Audit Logs
